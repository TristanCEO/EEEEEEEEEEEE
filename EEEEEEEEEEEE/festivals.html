Festivals of General Santos City

Tuna Festival

Celebrated every September 1 to 5, the Tuna Festival is the grandest event in GenSan. It features a colorful tuna-themed float parade, culinary competitions, seafood bazaars, street dancing, and various cultural presentations that highlight the city's status as the "Tuna Capital of the Philippines."

Kalilangan Festival

Held annually in February, the Kalilangan Festival showcases the rich cultural heritage of General Santos City. The festival includes street dancing, musical performances, traditional ceremonies, and activities that celebrate the harmonious coexistence of the city's diverse communities.

Paskuhan sa Gensan

Paskuhan sa Gensan is a festive Christmas celebration in December, featuring a grand lighting ceremony, Christmas bazaars, concerts, and community events that bring together families and friends to enjoy the holiday spirit in the city.

Yaman Gensan Festival

Yaman Gensan is a month-long celebration held every June, focusing on entrepreneurship, trade fairs, and investment forums. It aims to promote local businesses and showcase the economic growth and opportunities in General Santos City.
